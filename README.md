## 🚀 The Mission

This repository is the technical engine room for my **[Medium Blog](https://medium.com/@aghasyedi/list/planck-to-parsec-15e62a8e6b01)**. 

While my articles over on Medium focus on the big-picture storytelling of the universe, *this* is where we do the math. If you've ever read an article about Superconducting Qubits or Dyson Spheres and thought, *"Okay, but what does the Hamiltonian actually look like?"*—you are in the right place. 

We bridge the gap between pop science and peer-reviewed papers by focusing on:
* **First-Principles Derivations:** No "magic hand-waving" here. We build up complex models from foundational axioms.
* **The "Micro-Macro" Connection:** Finding the weird, beautiful symmetries between Quantum Information Science and High-Energy Astrophysics.
* **Explicit Formalism:** Clearly defining the metric tensors, approximations, and limits of every model we explore.

## 🗓️ The Weekend Flight Plan

To keep the research flowing without burning out the warp drive, we run on a strict weekend publication schedule:

* **Saturdays (The Deep Dives):** A single, comprehensive, LaTeX-heavy manuscript drops every Saturday. We alternate dynamically between **The Micro** (`/quantum`) and **The Macro** (`/astro`).
* **Sundays (The Sunday Hypotheses):** Reserved for the wilder stuff. I drop one (or sometimes a few) quick, 1-to-2-page theoretical expositions exploring the overarching frameworks and speculative boundaries of reality.

## 🗺️ Choose Your Domain

### ⚛️ The Micro (`/quantum` | Saturdays)
*Where reality gets fuzzy. Focusing on the physical realization of Quantum Information Processing.*
* **Superconducting Circuits:** The inner workings of SQUIDs, Fluxonium, and Transmons.
* **Quantum Optics:** Squeezed States, SNSPDs, and Quantum Metrology.
* **Quantum Sensing:** Tapping into atomic clocks and NV Centers.

### 🔭 The Macro (`/astro` | Saturdays)
*Where gravity rules. Focusing on high-energy phenomena and advanced space concepts.*
* **Exotic Propulsion:** Beam-Powered Propulsion (Breakthrough Starshot) and Light Sails.
* **Megastructures:** The thermodynamics and optical signatures of Dyson Spheres.
* **Cosmic Tests:** Taking Bell’s Inequality (CHSH) and local realism to astronomical scales.

### 🌌 The Sunday Hypotheses (`/expositions` | Sundays)
*Short-form theoretical frontiers for your Sunday morning coffee reading.*
* **Scope:** Dark Matter candidates, Singularity theorems, the Holographic Principle, and deep speculative cosmology.

## 🧠 How It's Made: The HITL Workflow

I use a **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)** approach to write these papers:
1. **AI-Assisted Gathering:** Generative AI acts as my research assistant, helping cross-reference textbooks and aggregate notation. 
2. **Human-Powered Physics:** The AI output is *strictly* a draft. Every equation, interpretation, and conceptual claim is manually verified, calculated, and formatted by me. **I take final responsibility for the physics.**
