# 🔭 Planck to Parsec: Astro Directory (The Macro)

Welcome to the `/astro` subdirectory of the **planck-to-parsec** repository. 

This section serves as the formal technical backend for the "Macro" physical regime. It houses the rigorous mathematical formalism, explicit physical assumptions (e.g., General Relativistic limits), and comprehensive notes that underpin the weekly **Saturday Astrophysical Hypotheses** publications.

The manuscripts archived here focus on high-energy phenomena and advanced space concepts, with active research spanning:
* **Exotic Propulsion:** The physics of Beam-Powered Propulsion (e.g., Breakthrough Starshot) and Light Sails.
* **Megastructures:** Thermodynamics and optical signatures of Dyson Spheres.
* **Fundamental Tests:** Bell’s Inequality (CHSH) and tests of local realism in cosmology.

## 📝 Maintenance & Publication Guide

To maintain the structural integrity and academic rigor of this directory, please adhere to the following conventions when archiving new manuscripts:

1. **Serial Numbering:** Always use a 4-digit, zero-padded serial number (`0000`, `0001`, `0002`, `0010`, etc.) in the `Index` column to ensure chronological scalability.
2. **File Naming:** Ensure the file names in the `File Link` column exactly match the uploaded files (including capitalization). Use `%20` for spaces in URLs (e.g., `Beam-Powered%20Propulsion.pdf`).
3. **Descriptions:** Keep the descriptions concise to ensure the table renders efficiently on smaller screens.
4. **Rigorous Verification:** In accordance with the repository's methodology, ensure all drafted metric tensors, physical interpretations, and conceptual claims in new uploads have undergone strict manual verification before being indexed here.
