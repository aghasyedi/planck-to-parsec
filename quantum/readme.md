# ⚛️ Planck to Parsec: Quantum Directory (The Micro)

Welcome to the `/quantum` subdirectory of the **planck-to-parsec** repository. 

This section serves as the formal technical backend for the "Micro" physical regime. It houses the rigorous, first-principles derivations, explicit mathematical formalism, and comprehensive notes that underpin the weekly **Saturday Quantum Insights** publications.

The manuscripts archived here focus on the physical realization of Quantum Information Processing, with active research spanning:
* **Superconducting Circuits:** The physics of SQUIDs, Fluxonium, and Transmon architectures.
* **Quantum Optics:** Squeezed States, SNSPDs, and Quantum Metrology.
* **Quantum Sensing:** Implementations such as Nitrogen-Vacancy (NV) Centers and Atomic Clocks.

## 📝 Maintenance & Publication Guide

To maintain the structural integrity and academic rigor of this directory, please adhere to the following conventions when archiving new manuscripts:

1. **Serial Numbering:** Always use a 4-digit, zero-padded serial number (`0000`, `0001`, `0002`, `0010`, etc.) in the `Index` column to ensure chronological scalability.
2. **File Naming:** Ensure the file names in the `File Link` column exactly match the uploaded files (including capitalization). Use `%20` for spaces in URLs (e.g., `Squeezed%20Light.pdf`).
3. **Descriptions:** Keep the descriptions concise to ensure the table renders efficiently on smaller screens.
4. **Rigorous Verification:** In accordance with the repository's methodology, ensure all drafted equations, Hamiltonians, and conceptual claims in new uploads have undergone strict manual verification before being indexed here.
